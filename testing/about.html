BC Master Guides is a leadership development program for youth and young adults in British Columbia. We provide training, mentorship, and certification for individuals seeking to serve in the Master Guide Ministry.

The Master Guide program is the highest level of leadership training within the Pathfinder and Adventurer ministries. It equips individuals with skills in leadership, outdoor survival, spiritual growth, and community service.

Mission

"To empower leaders through effective training, equipping them for service to God and humanity."

Vision

"To develop Christ-like leaders who will mentor, train, and disciple others for service in the Seventh-day Adventist Church and the community."
